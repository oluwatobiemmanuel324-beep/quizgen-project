Your Privacy Matters

QuizGen is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Analytics & Advertisers

We collect anonymous usage data (with your explicit consent) to improve the product and optionally provide aggregated insights to advertisers. Collected data may include age range, country/city (if provided), device type, active hours, interests (optional), and engagement summaries. We do not collect personally-identifying information without explicit permission. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing site data or in your account settings.

Data Collection

We collect and store the following information:

Data Type Storage Location Purpose
Quiz attempts Local device Track progress
Chat messages Local device Group communication
User preferences Local device Customize experience
Uploaded notes Local device Quiz generation

Local Storage

We prioritize your privacy by storing data locally on your device. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device by default
  • You control when to backup to our servers
  • You can clear local data at any time

Data Backup

When you choose to backup your data:

  • Data is encrypted during transfer
  • Backups are stored securely
  • You can delete backups at any time

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of backups
